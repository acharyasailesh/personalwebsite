    What is Risk???

    “Risk are future uncertain events with a probability of occurrence and a potential for loss.

    1. Possibility of suffering from loss in software development process is called a software risk.
    2. Increase in production cost, development of poor quality software, not being able to complete the project on time are some Software risk examples.
    3. Software risk exists because the future is uncertain and there are many known and unknown things that cannot be incorporated in the project plan.

    Causes of Risk

    1. Lack of information
    2. Lack of Control
    3. Lack of time and project management.

    Types of Risk

    1. Schedule Risk
    2. Budget Risk
    3. Operational Risk.
    4. Technical Risk
    5. Programmatic Risk.

    Schedule Risk

    1. Project schedule get slip when project tasks and schedule release risks are not addressed properly.
    2. Affect on project and finally on company economy and may lead to project failure.
    3. Schedules often slip due to following reasons:
      1. Wrong time estimation of Project.
      2.  Resources (staff, systems, skills of individuals) are not tracked properly.  Failure to identify complex functionalities and time required to develop those functionalities.
      3.  Unexpected project scope expansions.

      Solution

      Make good schedule of project and do it on time.

    Budget Risk

    1. Risk associated with the value of Money is known as Budget Risk.
        2. Causes:
      1. Wrong budget estimation.
      2.  Cost overruns
      3. Project scope expansion

      Solution

      Proper planning should be done for Budget Allocation.

    Operational Risk

    1. Also known as Procedural Risks
    2. Risk associated with the day-to-day operational activities of the project.
    3. Causes
      1. Lack of team spirit.
      2. Lack of clarity and failure to resolve the responsibilities
      3.   Insufficient resources
      4. No proper subject training
      5. No resource planning
      6. No communication in team.
        7.      

      Solution

      Make a proper understanding in team and have a team-spirit.

    Technical Risk

    1. Also known as Functional or Performance Risk.
    2. These are technical risks associated with the functionality of the software or with respect to the software performance.
    3. Causes
      1. Continuous changing requirements
      2. No advanced technology available or the existing technology is in initial stages.
      3.  Product is complex to implement.
      4. No proper subject training
      5. Difficult project modules integration.

      Solution

      Project members must know all factors necessary for software performance and have team work.

    Programmatic Risks:

    These are the external risks beyond the operational limits. These are all uncertain risks are outside the control of the program.
  • Causes
    1. Running out of fund.
    2. Market development
    3. Changing customer product strategy and priority
    4. Government rule changes.
